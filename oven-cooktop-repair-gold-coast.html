Oven & Cooktop Repair on the Gold Coast

A faulty oven or cooktop can bring your kitchen to a standstill. GC Appliance PRO provides same-day oven and cooktop repairs across the Gold Coast, handling everything from simple element replacements to complex control board faults.

Common Oven & Cooktop Faults

  • Not heating or uneven heating — Usually caused by a failed heating element (top, bottom, or fan-forced element).
  • Thermostat failure — Oven overheating, underheating, or temperature fluctuating indicates a faulty thermostat or temperature sensor.
  • Fan motor failure — Fan-forced ovens rely on a circulation fan. If it stops, heat distribution becomes uneven.
  • Door hinge or seal problems — A damaged door seal allows heat to escape, reducing efficiency and cooking performance.
  • Cooktop element not heating — Individual cooktop elements can fail and need replacement.
  • Control board/timer faults — Electronic controls that are unresponsive or erratic may need board-level repair.

As a Licensed Electrical Contractor

Oven and cooktop repairs involve mains-voltage electrical work. GC Appliance PRO holds a current Queensland Electrical Contractor Licence, ensuring all repairs meet Australian electrical safety standards. This is not just best practice — it’s a legal requirement for any electrical appliance work.
