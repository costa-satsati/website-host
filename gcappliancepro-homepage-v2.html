Common Questions About Our Service

Everything you need to know before booking an appliance repair.

Standard service call-out including diagnostics is $135 AUD, which covers up to 30 minutes on site. For complex faults or premium brands (Miele, Asko, Bosch, Siemens, Neff, Fisher & Paykel, LG), extended diagnostics is $165 AUD. The diagnostic fee is applied toward the total repair cost if you proceed with the repair.

Yes, Saturday appointments are available and are actually one of our busiest days. Many customers prefer Saturday service as it fits their work schedule. Book early in the week to secure your preferred time slot.

Standard labour is charged at $35 AUD per 15-minute block ($140 AUD per hour) after the initial diagnostic service call. You’ll always be quoted for any additional parts or labour before work begins.

Yes, we can install parts you’ve purchased yourself. Please note that we cannot provide a warranty on customer-supplied parts — our warranty only covers parts sourced through our own suppliers.

We provide a 3-month warranty on all labour. Parts carry the manufacturer’s warranty, which is typically 3 months. If the same fault reoccurs within the warranty period, we’ll return and repair at no additional charge.
