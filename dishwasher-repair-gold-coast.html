Expert Dishwasher Repair on the Gold Coast

Dishwasher not cleaning properly, leaking onto the floor, or flashing error codes? GC Appliance PRO diagnoses and repairs all types of dishwashers across the Gold Coast, from basic models to premium European brands.

Common Dishwasher Faults We Repair

  • Not cleaning dishes properly — Often caused by a faulty wash motor, blocked spray arms, or a worn impeller.
  • Leaking — Water on the floor usually indicates a worn door seal, cracked sump, or loose hose connection.
  • Won’t drain — Blocked drain pump, kinked drain hose, or a faulty drain pump motor.
  • Error codes — Each brand uses different error code systems. We can diagnose all major brands.
  • Not starting or powering on — May be a door latch issue, control board fault, or wiring problem.
  • Noisy operation — Worn bearings in the wash motor or foreign objects caught in the pump.

Brands We Service

We service dishwashers from Bosch, Miele, Fisher & Paykel, Smeg, Electrolux, LG, Samsung, Westinghouse, Asko, Siemens, and Neff. We have particular expertise with European premium brands which often require specialist diagnostic knowledge.
