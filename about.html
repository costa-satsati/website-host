About GC Appliance PRO

One Call. One Technician. No Runaround.

I’m the owner and sole operator of GC Appliance PRO, based in Labrador on the Gold Coast. With over 25 years of hands-on experience in appliance diagnostics and repair, I’ve built my reputation on honest assessments, fair pricing, and getting the job done right the first time.

Unlike national call centres where you never know who’s turning up, when you call GC Appliance PRO, you speak directly to me — the same person who will diagnose and repair your appliance. I specialise in all major household brands and pride myself on old-fashioned service: I turn up on time, I respect your home, and I won’t upsell you on repairs you don’t need.

I hold a current Queensland Electrical Contractor Licence and carry full Consumer Protection Insurance, so you can have complete confidence in the safety and quality of every repair.
