About Our Service Area

GC Appliance PRO is based in Labrador on the Gold Coast and provides mobile on-site appliance repairs to homes and businesses across the entire Gold Coast region. As a mobile service, we come to you — no need to transport heavy appliances.

Our core service area covers all suburbs from Sanctuary Cove and Hope Island in the north through to Burleigh Heads and Palm Beach in the south, including the hinterland suburbs of Nerang and Mudgeeraba.

Not sure if we cover your area? Give us a call on 0424 722 892 and we’ll let you know.
