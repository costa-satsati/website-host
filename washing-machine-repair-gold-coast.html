Professional Washing Machine Repair on the Gold Coast

Common Washing Machine Faults We Repair

  • Door seal (gasket) replacement — Leaking around the door is often caused by a torn or mouldy door seal. We carry common seals and can source specific models quickly.
  • Drum bearing failure — Loud rumbling or grinding during the spin cycle usually indicates worn drum bearings. We replace both inner and outer bearings along with the shaft seal.
  • Drain pump blockages and failures — If your machine won’t drain or is making a humming noise, the drain pump may be blocked or faulty.
  • Control board (PCB) faults — Error codes, machines stopping mid-cycle, or unresponsive controls can indicate a faulty control board.
  • Out-of-balance issues — Excessive vibration or movement during spin can be caused by worn shock absorbers, suspension springs, or an uneven load.
  • Inlet valve problems — Machine not filling with water, or filling too slowly, often points to a faulty water inlet valve.
